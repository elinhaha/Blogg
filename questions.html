Can beginners use this website?

Answer: Yes of course! This is a site for everyone that wants to learn how to play the guitar and for everyone that is already playing. Everyone is welcome here :)

Who is your favourite artist?

Answer: I love many different bands, but one of my favourites right now would be the band "The Orion Experience".

Will these guides guarantee that I learn how to play the guitar?

Answer: Everything is up to you, it's your responsibility to practise and spend time on improvement. These guides will simply help and assist you along the way.
